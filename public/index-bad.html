Each EXCHANGE brings Vendors ‘Memes’ which bring ownership, voting power & credits for the gifts & services of others. We have individual & business members. Meme-holders are grouped together with other similar vendors in Production Societies, which compose the ‘corporation’ (L ‘corp’ = ‘body’) & are represented on the board. We charge approximately 7% per transaction as an Administration fee to the Vendor. We require that Vendors invest approximately 8% (Depending on meme ownership, volume sales & our specialized Production Societies are charged less) per transaction in Memes. MULTI-STAKEHOLDER MEMBERS – Our ‘indigenous’ (L ‘self-generating’) ‘economic’ (Greek ‘oikos’ = ‘home’) system is designed to include the whole community with every form of contribution people make to their essential goods & services economy accounted for through Time-based Human-Resource Accounting & ‘caucused’ (Iroquois = ‘grouping of like interests’). The following four stakeholder accounting modules for Consumers, Workers, Suppliers & Founders, represent the full-cycle of the economic cycle. This holistic indigenous accounting heritage is the long forgotten origin of both capitalism & cooperation, which fragment from it during the invasions, aggressions & murder of the colonial period up to & including our time. WORKER members own at least 20 Memes, investing expertise, time, knowledge, skills etc at less than 50% of their Compensation Package in Memes. YEARLY REGISTRATION FEE: Members are charged only one Registration/Record Fee per year equal to ? Meme (5$08). This fee includes membership in all Associations covered by this fee. MEMES (Memory of contributions) are equal in value to the current Quebec Minimum Wage (10$15/ hour in 2013) or the value of a basic hour of work for an untrained beginning worker, which is capable of supporting a Living-Wage. As Quebec’s Minimum Wage rises so does the value of the Meme. The Corporate Board may adjust the Meme’s value in consideration of an adequate Living Wage. Each Vendor is free to charge rates per hour or per task as they desire but we expect rates roughly following market rates for goods & services. Members invest/affiliate their Memes to accounts within each Association where their membership rights & benefits are received. Vendor Members are required to own a MINIMUM of one Meme (non-voting). Each stakeholder Association has a schedule of minimum & maximum Meme ownership & the rights associated with each level of Progressive Ownership. At minimums of: 2 memes, Consumers are voting members, 20 Memes, Workers are voting, 20 Memes, Suppliers are voting & 40 Memes, Founders are voting members. Each Meme provides a vote for voting members to express their will within each Association & through their elected Directors on the Corporate Board of Directors. MAXIMUM Meme ownership per person is determined to keep Popular Participatory ownership decision-making within each Association described within the Document ‘Sharing our Livelihood’.
